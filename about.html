I start with my "Why"

My passion for design kindled when I attended Jacobson Middle School. I applied for the Yearbook Class, and tose up to become the yearbook editor. I spent most of my years in Tehachapi, a small town most noted for it's state prison, the nearby "Tehachapi Loop," and the 7,000 windmill units that dot the landscape.

Years later, I moved to become a highly involved student at California State University San Marcos. Along with undergraduate studies in marketing, retail, product design and development, I am currently a marketing specialist intern at Associated Students Incorporated.

I am also serving as the Public Relations Chair for the Sigma Chi Fraternity. I serve the Greek Leadership Council as the Vice President of Internal Affairs; through this I aim to instill the ideals of Greek Unity and equality in representation. I give back to the students through my service in Student Philanthropy Council, and fill the role of Marketing Chair for the Alpha Lambda Delta Honors Society. I am also a part of Greek Intervarsity (Greek IV), and a graduate of the Tukwut Leadership Circle.
